About Azadamard Scouting

Founded in 1977, Homenetmen Pasadena “Azadamard” Scouting has proudly carried forward a tradition of scouting, leadership, and Armenian heritage for generations of youth in our community. Through camping trips, outdoor skills, cultural education, and community service, Azadamard continues to shape confident, responsible young leaders who are proud of their roots and prepared to serve their communities.

New Scouts are always welcome.
